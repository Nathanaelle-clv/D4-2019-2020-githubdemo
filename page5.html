4 When a bee steals, pollen sticks on its hind legs, by going on another flower they deposit the powder of the preceding flower and thus help to their multiplication.

Save the bees! Do you know that bees tend to disappear? It is true, since about ten years, one observes a decrease of the number of colonies and a weakening. Bees are very important to our environment. They participate in the life of nature, and are part of what is called the food chain. No bees, no more fruits, no plants that feed humans, but also many animal species.
